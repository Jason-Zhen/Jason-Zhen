Product Management
As a product manager, I take pride in ensuring that the products I manage are both in line with company visions and of especial importance to me, is that they provide a satisfying user experience. User experience is often a neglected part of the modern corporation dominated environment but it is not dead yet and many successful companies still employ product management to prioritize the optimization of user experience. (See League of Legends Project below)
Video Game Enthusiast

Projects
Product Management: Analysis of the Success of League of Legends
League of Legends is a widely successful and can be argued to be the most successful game in today's video game market. Despite not using a heavy microtransaction business model, they have been able to make heavy profits and are successful on the business front as well as the game front. This project seeks to explain the User Experience explanation behind it.
Product Argument against Criticism of "Every Bootstrap Website Ever"
Bootstrap Template Websites have on occassion been criticized by designers (Example Link), however in accordance with design rules, this is not necessarily a fair criticism. It is true that these websites lack originality and are thus boring design-wise but they do satisfy the main criteria of designing a website in that it is able to convey information its intended audience in such a way that is both memorable and satisfying.
